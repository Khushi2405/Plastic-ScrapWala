    Frequently Answered Question

  • General
    • 1) Why I am not able to receive OTP during Registration on the App?
      It may be happening due to network or server issue. We request you to contact customer care at 82919 02545 of Plastic-ScrapWala mobile App.
    • 2) I forgot my login password? What should I do?
      On the login page, there is ‘Forgot Password’ option. Click on this to enter registered mobile number to receive the password on SMS.
    • 3) Why I am not able to log-in?
      If you have changed your mobile device or have Logged out of the App – then you need to call customer care at 82919 02545 of Plastic-ScrapWala mobile App to reset your device number. Post this you will be able to log in.
    • 4) I am not able to make an online payment to subscribe to the App?
      Contact customer care of Plastic-ScrapWala mobile App at 82919 02545 to get bank details for doing an offline payment. Also, bank details are available on Subscription Plan page of the mobile App.
  • Domestic Price
    • 1) Why are all polymer prices being updated of the Gujarat state in the domestic price section?

      Gujarat is one of the biggest markets of plastic scrap traders, manufacturers and recyclers. We provide the major market price that governs other state pricing. The App has recently started giving information on all types of plastic scrap prices in the Domestic Price module.

      In upcoming versions, we will add some more locations like Delhi and Tamil Nadu, so users will be able to check prices in others states as well.

    • 2) What are your sources for price updates?

      The prices are updated by confirmation with many industry participants like suppliers, traders, manufacturers (i.e., via market consensus) where large number of trades are done. To add, we get cross confirmation from Live! Buy Sell Offers by market participants on the Plastic-ScrapWala mobile App

    • 3) Why is my price not matching with Plastic-ScrapWala Mobile App?
      The price depends on (i) material quality (ii) payment terms and (iii) location. Kindly read our Quality Norms and Payment Assumptions given in Domestic Price section of the Plastic-ScrapWala mobile App.
  • Domestic News
    • 1) What information is given in domestic news section?

      In domestic news section plastic scrap and reprocessed granules, demand and supply situation news is updated. It’s a digital newspaper where news about PP, HD, LD, PET and all types of plastic scrap is been covered. With the help of the news section, you will be able to understand market trends to help take business decisions.

  • Live! Buy and Sell Offers
    • 1) How can I post my offer in LIVE Buy Sell offers section?

      There are two options available for posting your requirement: (a) On the Main Menu screen, at the bottom, by clicking on 'Post your requirement' button. (b) In LIVE Buy Sell offers section on right bottom side ‘Post Now' button.

    • 2) How can I find current Buyers and Sellers to know their requirement?

      At the top, you can see 2 buttons 'Live Buy Offers' & 'Live Sell Offers'. So, if you want to find Buyers then click on 'Live Buy Offers' by doing this you will see some offers below who want to buy your Product. If you want to find Sellers click on 'Live Sell Offers' by doing this you will see some offer below who want to sell their Product.

    • 3) How can I contact the person who has Posted?
      By clicking on ‘Give Offer’ WhatsApp button you can contact them as it will directly take you to WhatsApp.
    • 4) Why can't I see Offers for plastic scrap that I deal in?
      It may be there are no Live! Buyers or Sellers currently for your product. We recommend that you Post your requirement so that people can reach out to you.
    • 5) After posting how would I come to know whether someone is trying to contact me or not?
      You will get a message directly on your WhatsApp.
    • 6) What does Verified & Unverified tag indicate?

      Verified means the customer has submitted their GST CERTIFICATE to our team,and we have cross checked their details and GST returns filing time. After this procedure we have given them a tag as a Verified user of our app.

      Unverified tag indicates the user have not submitted their GST CERTIFICATE to our team.

  • Directory
    • 1) Why am I unable to contact the anyone in Plastic-ScrapWala directory?

      As of now the directory section is only available for subscribed users, you can contact them post subscription for the Directory related Plan.

    • 2) How can I list my company in Plastic-ScrapWala directory?

      Anyone who is in the plastic recycling industry can share their GST certificate to the Plastic-ScrapWala team for verification and post this verification they will be listed in the Directory.

  • International Price and News
    • 1) What information is given in International Price & News Section?

      It is an indicator of upcoming virgin prices. On every Saturday PTA, MEG, and Melt cost is updated. Also, you can watch Future Price of PP, PVC, LLDPE and many more.

      In the International News Section you can learn about international demand supply, up-down trends and other information. Also you can get instant notifications on the change of Oil prices.

  • Exihibition Cener
    • 1) What is Exhibition Center?

      This is an online exhibition center where you can exhibit your Products, Machines, Tools and Consumable relevant to plastic recycling. It is the right place for marketing to a relevant audience 24x7 saving cost and time.

    • 2) How can I exhibit my Product?

      In the section itself, you can see a ‘List Now’ blue button in the bottom right corner. By clicking it, and following the procedure as the app directs, you will be able to exhibit your Product.

    • 3) How many Products can I Exhibit at a time?
      You can exhibit as many products as you want for a nominal cost per product listing. As this is a ‘No commission’ exhibition platform.
