About Us

Plastic-ScrapWala, is an early-stage technology start-up, founded to support those who depend on the plastic scrap recycling to earn a living in India. By providing access to business-critical information and market-linkages, we equip players with the tools to make better decisions that will grow their businesses. Our offerings cater to players across the polymer recycling value chain from raw materials to consumables as well as associated offerings such as equipment and machinery. Thus, this platform is the first digital ecosystem for the recycled polymer market of India.

This mobile application is developed by Kalpataru Polymer Private Limited, based out of Mumbai, India.


Mission

Create a holistic digital ecosystem for the polymer recycling market in India by providing free-flow of information, fostering a flourishing marketplace and meeting all the needs of these players through one application.


Vision

Unite all players of the recycled plastic scrap ecosystem in India on this single platform, enabling them with information and market-access to grow their businesses, to increase the amount of plastic recycled in India.
